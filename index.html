Text Adventure MUD


This is an early (WIP) text-based, Open World (ASCCI) CRPG, where you define the fate of your story! Choose from over 30 Hair Styles, 17 Hair Colours, and 6 Eye Colours in the Character Creator. Roll play your way through a simulated world comprised of interactable environments, items, and NPCs. NPCs have their own scheduals; pay attention to the time of day, and the weather, as both could be an obstacle to your objectives. Perhaps you'll overcome some limitations with criminal mischief? With a simulated Crime system, you can do just that! Watch out for Police and other Law Enforcement if you plan on breaking any laws; and watch your back! Local Gangs and Cliques vie for control of different regions and other various locations.

What is TA MUD?

TA MUD is an ASCCI, Terminal Based, Standalone MUD constructed in Python. TA MUD was built from the ground up. All internal workings are products of TA MUD development and testing.
Any snippits referenced during R&D were reverse engineered and adapted specifically to suit TA MUD use cases.

Automatic Updates

The Standalone TA MUD Launcher will stay up to date with the latest releases; so you can get the best out of TA MUD's new content developments and bug fixes.

Simulated Ecosystem

The wildlife is made out of wildlife. TA MUDs creatures (so far) possess a visual cortex and various hypocampal formations, allowing them to make intelligent decisions as well as form memories and communicate amungst themselves.
The implementation of better AI neural processess will be advanced over time.
