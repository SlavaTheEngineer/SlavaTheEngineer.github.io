Text Adventure MUD


This is an early (WIP) text-based, Open World CRPG, where you define the fate of your story! Choose from over 30 Hair Styles, 17 Hair Colours, and 6 Eye Colours in the Character Creator. Roll play your way through a simulated world comprised of interactable environments, items, and NPCs. NPCs have their own scheduals; pay attention to the time of day, and the weather, as both could be an obstacle to your objectives. Perhaps you'll overcome some limitations with criminal simulated Crime system, you can do just that! Watch out for Police and other Law Enforcement if you mischief? With a plan on breaking any laws; and watch your back! Local Gangs and Cliques vie for control of different regions and other various locations; will you become their enemy or an ally. Who will you become?

Dive in to Text Adventure and experience a CRPG set in the year 2070, . . .
